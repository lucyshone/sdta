About

Synergy Dance Training Academy was established 6 years ago in the West Midlands, and currently has students ranging from 6-25 years old. We provide the opportunity to be involved in competitions and examinations, both of which help the students progress and develop their skills and technique. To read more about Synergy, click here.

Classes

Synergy offers a range of classes for all ages. We offer classes that cover multiple styles of dance and support many different abilities, from beginners to advanced dancers. For more information about the styles we offer, or to view our timetable, click here.
